Project Overview

-Designed and implemented a sentiment analysis model to evaluate customer reviews from e-commerce platforms. -Leveraged advanced techniques like Bag-of-Words, TF-IDF, and maximum entropy to convert reviews into vector representations. -Utilized Python libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Word Cloud to analyze a large-scale Amazon dataset. -Achieved high accuracy in sentiment classification, efficiently polarizing reviews. -Provided actionable insights into customer feedback, enhancing product evaluation and decision-making processes.
